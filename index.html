ABOUT US

We are the IWSC Banasthali, the student chapter of Inception Wave from Banasthali Vidyapith. A student-based multi-centric dynamic team, we organize and consolidate events, conferences and social outreach and provide a common roof for enterprising, unique and creative minds to assemble and work in consonance with integrity and vision.

Our Vision:

  • Associate banasthalites with the core of Banasthali.
  • Provide platform to exhibit a variety of talents.
  • Banding together with Banasthali clubs and teams as a media partner.
  • Inculcate/Provide industry level exposure.

At IWSC Banasthali, we galvanize around modern themes and trends, produce engaging, informative, and authentic content, and celebrate the common college themes of fun, studies, campus life and enriching activities through relatable and well-planned initiatives.
