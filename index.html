ABOUT US

We are the IWSC Banasthali, the student chapter of Inception Wave from Banasthali Vidyapith. A student-based multi-centric dynamic team, we organize and consolidate events, conferences and social outreach and provide a common roof for enterprising, unique and creative minds to assemble and work in consonance with integrity and vision.

Our Vision:

  • Associate banasthalites with the core of Banasthali.
  • Provide platform to exhibit a variety of talents.
  • Banding together with Banasthali clubs and teams as a media partner.
  • Inculcate/Provide industry level exposure.

At IWSC Banasthali, we galvanize around modern themes and trends, produce engaging, informative, and authentic content, and celebrate the common college themes of fun, studies, campus life and enriching activities through relatable and well-planned initiatives.

शुभारंभ - An auspicious beginnings

2020

Stage, lights and camera set-up, a nerve-wracking thought appeared whether contestants and audiences would be interested to show up in the first place? But guess what...we marked our beginning with the best. With an aim to bring the best talents in the college and to help boost the self-esteem, confidence, and assurance of youth Iwsc banasthali community organized an event to discovere hidden talents.

With the help and coordination of the team members a successful event in which participants performed the art of singing,dancing and poetry took place. More than 200 people attended the show including staff, Dean, HoD and other students. It felt great. We recieved appreciation from each and every one present there. To sum it all up it was an amazing experience and we learnt a lot.
